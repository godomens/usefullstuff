Security

Password Manager

KeepassXC. It is a free open-source password manager. You will have to store the file on a cloud service like Dropxbox or Google Drive in order to use it across several devices. Unforntunately it does not work yet on Proton Drive. As the file itself is encrypted I do not worry so much about it and store it on Dropbox.

ProtonMail

I stepped over from Gmail to the Proton "universe". Like Google, Proton has its own privacy focused AI, Drive and Email. As they promise to not collect any data and sell or use it, you have of course to pay for it. You will find it here: Proton Mail Remember: Nothing is free, you will "pay" them by giving them your data! Especially with AI, companies may one day be able to price-gouge products based on what they know you like! Think of travel routes, hobby products, clothing, everything!

Painting

Realistic Painting

Visit the website drawmixpaint.com for a complete realistic oil painting course. It is free!

Important: I do NOT use any harmful spirits or oil paint like suggested in this course. I only use linseed oil (not refined!) and series 1 of Winsor and Newton. For prepping my canvas with burned siena I use watermixable oil paints.

If you buy professional oil paint (higher seriesnumbers), you risk buying oil paints containing the "original/real" pigments, like cadmium, which are very toxic! Always inform yourself beforehand by searching on the suppliers website for the SDS (safety data sheet) of your oil paint!

For some paintings I already switched to watersoluble oil paints, which are non toxic and very suitable for beginners! Highly recommend them! Any brand like Winsor and Newton Artisan, Cobra or Schmincke Norma is fine.
